My cat, named Juno, is pictured with a Mexican tequila and French herbal liquor, and dances in a local Los Angeles club. Juno dons a mustard-yellow Minions cap--invented in France, franchised in the US, manufactured in China. The music is electronic and fast-paced, featuring layered thumping drumlines and synths, simply electric! The songs featured at the club are drum n bass and breakcore, both originating from working class Jamaican immigrants in the UK.

In real life, Juno has an affinity for silver vine and catnip, which flings her into a fit of spinning, kicking, and rolling... not too different from your local hardcore scene. Inspired by her proclivities for stimulants, I inserted her into an electronic dance club, brimming with the energy
